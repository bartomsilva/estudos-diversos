OFFERS

Zoni Language Centers offers students English exam preparation courses for TOEFL iBT, IELTS and Cambridge ESOL exams. They cover all the integrated English skills and techniques in taking the actual exams.

In addition, the course is focused on the strategies and techniques needed to successfully obtain a score needed to be admitted to college or university of your own choice in the United States and the United Kingdom.

Furthermore, the lectures, discussions and practice tests are held under real test conditions and facilitated by qualified ESL teachers with a high knowledge in exam preparation courses.

By the end of any of our Zoni exam preparation courses, you will be fully prepared to take an internationally distinguished exam. We currently offer exam preparation for IELTS, TOEFL iBT and Cambridge (PET, FCE, CAE, CPE).

TOEFL iBT TEST PREPARATION

Course Description

The TOEFL iBT measures the English language skills of second language learners or non-native English speakers. As part of the admission requirements of colleges and universities, students are required to have an advanced level of English proficiency and a high score in TOEFL ibT. The TOEFL iBT preparation course focuses on effective techniques and strategies in taking the Internet-based test (iBT). This includes practice tests with integrated speaking, listening, reading and writing tasks. In addition, students study grammar, build their vocabulary, learn idiomatic expressions and practice pronunciation.

CAMBRIDGE ESOL EXAM PREPARATION COURSE

Course Description

This course is designed to prepare students to pass the Cambridge First Certificate Exam (FCE), the Cambridge Advanced Exam (CAE) and the Cambridge Proficiency Exam (CPE). These exams are internationally recognized for work, study and travel abroad. The Cambridge preparation course includes strategies and techniques for each of the 5 components of the test – listening, speaking, reading, writing and use of English. This course also helps students to rapidly improve vocabulary, grammar, and pronunciation as well as developing skills that can be used for other English proficiency exams.

IELTS PREPARATION

Course Description

International English Language Testing System ( IELTS) Preparation

This course helps students to develop the skills and strategies needed to pass the IELTS exam, as well as other English proficiency tests. There are four parts to the IELTS exam: Listening, Reading, Writing and Speaking. All students take the same Listening and Speaking exams, while the Reading and Writing parts are available in both Academic and General formats. The Academic Reading and Writing exam assess whether a student is able to study a course taught in English. The General exam looks at basic English skills in broad social and educational contexts. The General exam suits students who are going to English-speaking countries for work, non-degree level training or for immigration. The course includes vocabulary, grammar, skill-building and exam practice. It consists of four modules with corresponding exercises, tasks and practice tests.
